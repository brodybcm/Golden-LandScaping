# Google Business Profile - Service Descriptions

### Lawn Mowing & Edging
Maintain a perfect lawn with our precision mowing and edging services. We cut your grass to the ideal height, trim all edges cleanly, and blow off all hard surfaces for a pristine, professional finish.

### Tree Pruning
Keep your trees healthy and safe with our expert pruning. We remove dead or overgrown branches to improve tree structure, enhance safety, and boost your property's overall curb appeal.

### Shrub Trimming
Shape and maintain your hedges and shrubs. We provide detailed trimming to promote healthy plant growth and keep your landscape looking neat, balanced, and professionally managed.

### Mulching
Protect and beautify your flower beds with our premium mulch installation. Our mulch retains vital soil moisture, suppresses weeds, and instantly refreshes the look of your entire landscape.

### Weed Control
Eliminate stubborn weeds from your lawn and flower beds. We use targeted treatments to remove invasive plants and prevent them from returning, ensuring a thick, healthy yard.

### Yard Cleanups
Restore your property with our comprehensive seasonal cleanups. We remove fallen leaves, clear overgrown debris, and properly prepare your landscape for the changing seasons.
